The Inner Talent Group-ITG is an organization that helps migrants that are new to Dutch society to find their place in the Netherlands. Our primary objective is to help these new Dutchies (as we like to call them) to find and develop their own talents. A lot of immigrants arrive in the Netherlands with a lot of ambitions, talents and plans, but with no possibilities to pursue their dreams or make use of their skillsets. We believe this to be an immense waste of talent!

By tapping into the talents of new Dutchies our projects help to connect new dutchies with old dutchies and bridge the gap between migrants and society.
